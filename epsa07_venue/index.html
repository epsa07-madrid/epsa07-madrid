epsa 2007: the venue

The Venue

   EPSA 2007 is held at the Faculty of Philosophy of Complutense University, Madrid. The Complutense University is currently ranked the top university in Spain and it is one of the oldest universities in the world, having been founded in 1293 as Estudio de las Escuelas Generales de Alcalá. Over 100,000 students are currently registered and taught by nearly 10,000 staff.

   The conference is held in the Filosofía "A" building, located in the north-west of the Complutense Campus (see the Campus map).

   The building is marked "Fac. de Filosofía", directly opposite the Faculty of Law, "Fac. de Derecho". Information on how to reach the venue can be found in the travel information section in this website.

   The sessions will be held in the rooms "Paraninfo", "Salón de Grados", "Sala de Juntas", "Seminario A217" and "Seminario A25" (see the building map). The keynote lectures will take place in the "Paraninfo", a lecture theatre with 300 seats.

   All sessions will be in English, the official language of EPSA07.

   More information regarding the venue will be posted here nearer the time.
