conference registration & programme

Conference Registration

   To register simply download the attached registration form and fill it in accordance with your needs. Then post it, fax it or scan-and-email it to the address at the top of the form. Alternatively, you may hand in this form at the registration desk in the Conference foyer.

   There is a cap of 300 delegates max. at the conference, and registrations will be accepted on a first-come-first-serve basis until this cap is reached.

Conference Programme

   The conference will start on Wednesday 14 November at 16:00 and will finish on Saturday 17 November at 17:00. The final programme of the meeting and the book of abstracts are now available.
